¿Que es ser Maestro de Taekwon-do

Es ser un eterno practicante, que disfruta mucho del entrenamiento, incluso en altas exigencias donde los dolores y golpes son una constante. También es ser Educador, que brinda su experiencia y “Método” de enseñanza a alumnos e Instructores. Un “Método” ordenado y sistematizado, en constante desarrollo y al alcance de los Instructores que conforman I.T.A.U, con el gran objetivo de poder enseñar en el menor tiempo posible, con el mejor resultado. La Comunicación, Liderazgo y Filosofía (“Do”), juegan un papel importantísimo. Es enseñar desde el ejemplo, en lo técnico y ético. Es una persona que siempre busca sacar lo mejor de cada practicante, desde el más pequeño al Instructor más avanzado.

His Story

In 1992, the current Master Javier del Castillo began his practice in Taekwon-do. However, in 1994, he started his training as a Master by teaching young 16-year-olds with a blue belt, a role he has performed for over 30 years. Fate led him to cross paths with General Choi Hong Hi during the first and only visit of the Taekwon-do founder to Uruguay in 1995. A year later, he participated in his second course with General Choi in Tucumán, Argentina IIC. From that moment on, his path was set and his destiny within Taekwon-do was clear.

Over these 30 years, he has dedicated himself to constant practice and training with the greatest Martial Arts experts, building an extensive sports career as a competitor and coach.

Present day

In present days, he remains active in training and preparing competitors at both the national and international levels. In addition to his role as a Taekwon-do master, he provides technical guidance to instructors from different countries through courses, in-person and virtual classes. Master Javier del Castillo and the group of instructors he leads continuously participate in training sessions encompassing all aspects of the Martial Art, collaborating with local and international experts. Their aim is to provide their practitioners with the best quality and experience in their practice.
